Thai Chicken Curry

Description

You can have this ready faster than takeout can arrive.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp vegetable or sunflower oil
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • 4 tsp Thai green curry paste
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 (12-ounce) can coconut milk
  • 2 tsp Thai fish sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 3 wide stips lime zest
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • handful of fresh basil leaves, torn, plus extra whole leaves for serving cooked white or brown rice for serving

Steps

  1. In a large frying pan, heat vegetable oil until very hot. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute
  2. Spoon in Thai green curry paste, and stir it around for a few seconds. Add the chicken and stir to cook thoroughly.
  3. Pour in the coconut milk and bring to a boil.
  4. Add Thai fish sauce, sugar, lime zest, and peas. Reduce heat and cook for 5 to 10 minutes.
  5. Add torn basil leaves, and serve with rices and basil.
