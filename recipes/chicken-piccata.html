Chicken Piccata

Description

It looks fancy, but it is so simple

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts. butterflied and halved
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 2 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 Tbsp capers
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 2 Tbsp finely chopped fresh flat parsley

Steps

  1. Cover chicken with wax paper, and pound each slice until even and thin.
  2. Combine flour with salt and pepper on a large plate. Dredge chicken pieces in the flour, and shake off.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil on high heat until very hot. Fry chicken for 2 to 3 minutes per side. Set aside and keep warm.
  4. In the same skillet, reduce heat and add the chicken stock, white wine, and lemon juice.
  5. Scrape the brown pieces from the bottom of the skillet, and add the capers.
  6. Bring to a boil until reduced by half. Then whisk in butter and parsley.
  7. Pour sauce over chicken pieces, and serve.
