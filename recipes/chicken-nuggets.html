Chicken Nuggets

Description

This chicken is best marinated, but if you don't have time, just let it sit in the buttermilk while you prepare everything else.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts. cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups cereal (oat or corn flakes)
  • 1/2 cup plain bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • salt and pepper
  • 4 Tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Place chicken in a large bowl, cover with buttermilk, and let marinate covered with plastic wrap in the refrigerator overnight.
  2. Preheat oven to 375° F.
  3. Before breading, add the egg to the marinade and mix to coat the chicken.
  4. Smash the cereal into crumbs using a food processo, or place the cereal in a resealable bag and roll with a rolling pin.
  5. Combine the cereal, bread crumbs, flour, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a medium bowl.
  6. Remove chicken from buttermilk, and dredge pieces in the dry mixture.
  7. Heat olive oil in a large saucepan. Fry the chicken in batches for 4 to 6 minutes, or until golden brown and crispy.
  8. Transfer to an ovenproof dish, and bake for 15 minutes.
